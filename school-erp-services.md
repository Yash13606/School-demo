# School ERP — Complete Services We Provide

---

## What We Build For You

We design, develop, and deliver a complete School Enterprise Resource Planning system — a single unified platform that connects every department, stakeholder, and process in your school. From admissions to examinations, fees to transport, everything runs from one place. One login. One dashboard. Everything connected.

---

## Who Gets Access

The platform is built on Role-Based Access Control. Every user sees only what they need — nothing more, nothing less.

- **School Administrator** — Full control over all school operations, users, academics, and finances
- **Teachers** — Classroom management, attendance marking, homework, marks entry, timetable, and leaves
- **Students** — Personal academic tracking, homework submission, results, timetable, and leave requests
- **Parents** — Child's fees, dues, transport details, exam results, and notifications
- **Accountant** — Fee collection, payment recording, receipts, and payroll
- **Librarian** — Book catalog, issue and return management, and fine tracking

- Every user's access is strictly controlled by their assigned role
- Parents can only see data belonging to their own child
